18+ powerful features designed for patients and healthcare providers alike.

AI Health Chat

Get instant health guidance powered by Claude AI. Ask about symptoms, medications, lab results, nutrition, and more.

Voice Conversations

Talk to your AI health assistant hands-free. Real-time speech recognition with natural voice responses in Arabic and English.

Lab Scanner (OCR)

Scan paper lab reports with your camera. AI-powered OCR digitizes and analyzes results instantly with trend tracking.

Medication Manager

Track medications, dosages, and schedules. Adherence calendars, smart reminders, drug interaction warnings, and dose logging.

Nutrition Tracking

Log meals, scan food barcodes, search USDA database. Track calories, protein, carbs, and fat with daily progress rings.

Apple Health Sync

Two-way sync with Apple Health. Import heart rate, blood pressure, weight, steps, and more. Your data stays connected.

Exercise Logging

Log walking, running, cycling, gym, swimming, and yoga. Automatic calorie estimation with weekly progress tracking.

Sleep Tracker

Monitor sleep duration, quality, and patterns. Visualize your sleep history with charts and set personalized sleep goals.

Family Health

Monitor health records for up to 6 family members. Share labs, medications, and vitals. Invite via link or QR code.

Vitals Dashboard

Track blood pressure, blood sugar, heart rate, temperature, weight, and oxygen. Visual trends and smart alerts for anomalies.

Health Passport

Export your complete health summary as a professional PDF. Share with doctors during visits, referrals, or emergencies.

Lab Directory

Find nearby laboratories with ratings and available tests. View locations on a map and book appointments directly.

Symptom Checker

AI-powered symptom analysis. Describe how you feel, get potential causes, urgency assessment, and guidance on next steps.

Smart Reminders

Custom medication alarms, lab follow-up reminders, and health check notifications. Never miss a dose or appointment.

AI Insights

Personalized health insights generated from your data. Actionable recommendations for nutrition, exercise, sleep, and medications.

Period Tracker

Track menstrual cycles with predictions, fertile windows, and symptom logging. Visual calendar with history and personalized cycle insights.

Social Connections

Connect with friends and family on the platform. Search users, send connection requests, and share health milestones with your circle.

Daily Health Tips

Receive personalized daily health tips and reminders. AI-curated advice on nutrition, exercise, mental wellness, and preventive care.
